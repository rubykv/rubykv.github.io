About

I hail from a small town in Kerala, India - Ottapalam.

From my school days, I have loved writing, trying my hands on long essays, poetry, and short stories.

I started my career as a programmer in 2008, working for consultancies and startups.

In 2015, I took a break from my career and started a digital magazine - Crazy Window. After running the business for close to 2 years I couldn’t make it profitable and had to shut down.

Later I took a job assignment in the USA and have since worked in the retail and cable industries, helping their technology department build and scale backend applications.

Once again, burdened with repeated and mundane tasks at work, I decided to pursue my passion for writing and building applications as a side hustle. Eventually, this helped me start a digital business by getting paid for my blogs and selling useful digital products.

I still take programming jobs on a contract basis and that’s my main source of income.

Apart from our careers, My husband and I love to travel and explore places. We have been to 5 countries so far on a well-planned budget trip and we wish to travel the world by adopting a 100% remote work lifestyle.

I have a Bachelor of Technology degree in Information Technology and an executive program certification in Design Thinking & Innovation.

If you need help with technical content writing or prototyping solutions, please get in touch. I would love to discuss the work with you.
